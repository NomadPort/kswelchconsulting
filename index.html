Welcome

In our office we provide hope, healing and ongoing support for our clients. Therapy is designed to target a variety of struggles and issues in a safe, therapeutic environment. We use an array of experiential techniques integrating mindfulness and interpersonal neurobiology to address individual styles and preferences. We work collaboratively with each client to understand what they need, making sure the experiences they share are held with compassion and care. We design comprehensive treatment plans that advocate individuals and families utilize all available resources including: teachers, guidance counselors, medical doctors and psychiatrists. Our office has a warm and friendly atmosphere where each clent is viewed as a unique, distinct individual human being to be valued and treated respectfully.
