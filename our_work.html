What We Do


Relationships heal. Behind every connection we have with others are memories that only our bodies know. Our early interpersonal experiences shape our brains, minds, and bodies throughout our lives. Interpersonal Neurobiology (IPNB) studies how the brain grows and is influenced by personal relationships. Quality sustained support and connection can reshape the pathways of our nervous system that have been impacted by relational misattunement, stress and trauma. Our work together will use processes that encourage embodied exploration through art, sand and body awareness. Deep change happens when we access those unsupportive, unconscious patterning through our bodies. My goal is to provide a safe, caring and gentle environment for you to heal.
