About Me

Karen Welch is a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist specializing in individual, couples and family therapy, and maintains a private practice in Tustin, California.

With over 15 years experience as a marriage and family therapist, and as a graduate of Trinity College specializing in Counseling Psychology, she is currently serving as private practitioner working with a broad spectrum of clients.

In addition to being a prominent relationship therapist, Karen participates in small intensive study groups focusing on interpersonal neurobiology.

Karen is an interactive, therapist. Her therapeutic approach is to provide support and practical feedback to help clients effectively address personal life challenges. She integrates complementary methodologies and techniques to offer a highly personalized approach tailored to each client. With compassion and understanding, she works with each individual to help them build on their strengths and attain the personal growth they are committed to accomplishing.


Education: BS Communication Disorders; MA Counseling Psychology

License: LMFT

Additional Training: Intensive training in Interpersonal Neurobiology with Bonnie Badenoch

Professiona Memberships: CAMFT; IPNB; Post Partum Support International
